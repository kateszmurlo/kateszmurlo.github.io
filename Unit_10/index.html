Hi, I'm Kate

My name is Kate. I'm a graphic designer living in New York City. Growing up in the suburbs of Chicago, I lived and breathed creativity. From an early age, I found myself bouncing around from a variety of art and creative writing classes. From ceramics to oil painting to comic drawing to architecture to paper making to watercolors, I was excited to create it all. It's an characteristic embedded into my personality, which I decided to pursue as a professional career. I hope to continue to learn, create, and travel as often as I can.
